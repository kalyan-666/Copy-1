MORE ABOUT US

BAtalks is a pioneer in discovering the immense potential of voice technology to revolutionize the way we interact with the world. Our passion lies in crafting innovative projects entirely driven by the power of voice.

Our mission is clear - To democratize access to the incredible capabilities of voice Technology. We envision a future where everyone can effortlessly harness the efficiency and convenience of voice-driven solutions in their daily lives. Driven by a relentless pursuit of innovation and a commitment to accessibility, BAtalks is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in the realm of voice technology. Join us on this exciting journey as we transform the way we connect, learn, and experience the world—through the power of voice.

Project Domains

MEDICAL

  • In the medical domain, we've successfully delivered two projects under the brand names LMC and OPA.
  • LMC (LocateMyCare) is a patient-focused app that helps users discover hospitals and book appointments with shorter wait times.
  • The solution demonstrates our capability to build user-centric, scalable healthcare platforms that enhance operational efficiency and patient experience.

EDUCATION

  • In Education domain we have four projects dedicated for CA, CMA, CS, Banking students under the brand of voice memorize.
